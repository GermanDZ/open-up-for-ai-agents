 A Trainer delivers training either to End Users of the system or to the production support personnel who are responsible for maintaining the system.

Main Description

Many organizations engage Trainers to deliver product training to various audiences. Unlike a software Developer, most Trainers have extensive experience in delivering training materials in a fashion that is well suited to communicating complex concepts to End Users. They bring a necessary creativity and talent that complements the implementation and testing skills of a development team.
